Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 8, 2026

Introduction

This application ("App") is a private/internal integration tool operated by ZENITH.AI for connecting Meta Ads data with automation and reporting workflows used by the App owner.

This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles data accessed through Meta APIs, including the Meta Marketing API.

Data Collection and Use

The App may access Meta Ads data only for advertising management, analysis, reporting, and optimization purposes. Depending on the permissions granted, this may include:

  • Meta ad account information
  • Campaign, ad set, ad, and creative configuration
  • Campaign performance metrics and insights
  • Page or business asset information needed to manage advertising workflows
  • Audiences and targeting information where required for campaign setup or analysis

The App uses this information solely to help the App owner manage and optimize advertising activity. Data is not sold, rented, or disclosed to third parties.

Data Storage

Data retrieved through the App is processed for operational use. The App may store limited local configuration, logs, cached API responses, or access tokens only when necessary for the App's functionality, debugging, or reporting workflows.

The App does not intentionally store unnecessary personal data. Cached data is removed when no longer needed for active operations.

Data Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect data accessed through the App, including secure API connections, restricted access to credentials and access tokens, local/environment-based secret management, and limiting requested permissions to the intended advertising use case.

Third-Party Services

This App integrates with Meta technologies, including Meta Ads / Marketing API. Use of Meta services is subject to Meta's own terms and privacy policies.

Meta Privacy Policy: https://www.facebook.com/privacy/policy/

Data Sharing

Data accessed through this App is used only by the App owner for advertising management and optimization. We do not sell or share data with advertising brokers, data brokers, or unrelated third parties.

User Control and Data Deletion

Users may request deletion of data associated with the App by following the instructions in the User Data Deletion page linked in the App settings.
